### National Audit of Pulmonary Hypertension


National Audit of Pulmonary Hypertension
Local Action Plan - 10th Annual Report

1
Pg
Standard
Recommendation
Planned action
35

90 per cent of patients who undergo a
pulmonary endarterectomy should wait
less than four months from their
diagnosis of chronic thromboembolic
pulmonary hypertension.

NHS England and PH centres need to
address the delay in undertaking
pulmonary endarterectomy. This
requires a joint effort since the patient
pathway is complex.

See Royal Papworth response

At a local level we continue to track patients through the
pathway and regularly audit those patients not meeting the
standards. We aim to adopt the proposed virtual MDT with Royal
Papworth Hospital to shorten the time to MDT decision
regarding suitability for surgery.
